Disability Support Worker in Melbourne

At Able Aide, we provide professional disability support workers who assist NDIS participants with daily living tasks, personal care, and independent living goals.

Whether you need help at home, in the community, or with managing your NDIS plan, our friendly team is here to support you.

What We Offer:

  • Professional Disability Support Workers – Trained and experienced in supporting individuals with a range of disabilities.
  • Home Care Support Services – From personal hygiene to meal preparation, our team helps clients live comfortably at home.
  • NDIS Support Coordination – We help participants manage their NDIS plans and connect with the right services.
  • Personal Care Assistant Services – Including mobility support, toileting, showering, and dressing.
  • Community Access Support – Helping clients attend appointments, go shopping, or participate in local events.
