Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our NDIS support services.

What is NDIS?

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) provides support to people with disabilities to achieve their goals and improve their quality of life.

Who can access NDIS services?

Anyone under the age of 65 who meets the eligibility criteria can access NDIS services. Eligibility is based on factors such as residency, disability type, and functional capacity.

How do I book a consultation?

Contact us via email or phone, and our team will guide you through the process. We’ll schedule a consultation to discuss your needs and create a personalized plan.

Do you operate Australia-wide?

Yes, we provide services across Australia, with a focus on Melbourne, Victoria. Our team is available to assist participants in various regions.

How much does it cost to access your services?

The cost of our services depends on your NDIS plan and funding allocation. We’ll work with you to ensure all services are covered within your plan.

What types of support do you offer?

We offer Core Supports (e.g., daily living assistance, personal care), Capacity Building (e.g., skill development, employment readiness), Support Coordination, and Plan Management.
