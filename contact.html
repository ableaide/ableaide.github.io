Frequently Asked Questions

What is NDIS?

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) provides support to people with disabilities to achieve their goals and improve their quality of life.

Who can access NDIS services?

Anyone under the age of 65 who meets the eligibility criteria can access NDIS services.

How do I book a consultation?

Contact us via email or phone, and our team will guide you through the process.

Do you operate Australia-wide?

Yes, we provide services across Australia, with a focus on Melbourne, Victoria.
